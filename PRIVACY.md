# Privacy Policy for GraphQL Mocker

**Last Updated:** December 2, 2025

## Overview

GraphQL Mocker is a browser extension designed to help developers capture, edit, and mock GraphQL API responses for testing and development purposes. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

GraphQL Mocker captures the following data **locally on your device only**:

- GraphQL request and response data from websites you visit
- Custom mock responses you create
- User preferences (such as dark mode setting and global toggle state)

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information
- Email addresses or contact information
- Location data
- Browsing history
- Passwords or authentication credentials
- Financial information
- Health information

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's Storage API.**

- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties
- No analytics or tracking is implemented
- Data remains on your device and under your control

## Data Usage

The collected data is used solely for:

- Displaying captured GraphQL operations in the extension popup
- Storing your custom mock responses for testing purposes
- Preserving your user preferences across browser sessions

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.**

## Data Retention

- Data is stored locally until you clear it using the "Clear All" button in the extension
- Data is automatically removed when you uninstall the extension
- You have full control over your data at all times

## Security

Since all data remains local to your browser:

- No network transmission of your data occurs
- No server-side vulnerabilities can affect your data
- Your data is as secure as your local browser storage

## User Rights

You have the right to:

- View all captured data within the extension
- Delete any or all data at any time
- Uninstall the extension to remove all associated data

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
